# LLM Selection Decision

| Decision Factor                 | My Choice | Reason (1 sentence) |
|---------------------------------|-----------|---------------------|
| Which LLM followed my prompt structure most faithfully? | ChatGPT 5.2 | It provided clear, layer-by-layer breakdowns that aligned with the teardown framework structure. |
| Which LLM was most technically accurate (least hallucination)? | ChatGPT 5.2 | Specificity scores of 5 across most layers with consistent real-tech naming and no made-up details. |
| Which LLM's output was most readable and well-organized? | ChatGPT 5.2 | Best structured output rating and "neat and clean" formatting that made insights clear and actionable. |
| Which LLM handled the "honesty check" best (admitting when a layer doesn't apply)? | ChatGPT 5.2 | Acknowledged limitations while providing thorough answers, showing integrity in technical assessments. |

**Selected LLM for final tool:** ChatGPT 5.2
**Why:** ChatGPT 5.2 dominates across all decision factors with the highest specificity ratings (averaging 4.3/5), least hallucination, best structure, and strongest technical insights across all six layers, making it the clear choice for comprehensive product teardowns.
